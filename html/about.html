About OnePay

With over 15 years of deployment experience, OnePay is the first company
providing digital payment solutions & services in Vietnam.

Offering diverse payment solutions for merchants, banks, financial institutions and payment intermediaries.

By connecting with local banks and International Card Associations, OnePay provides a comprehensive payment solution to help merchants and banks expand their business.

Safe and flexible solution

Our solution is complying with security requirements of banks and
International Card Associations.

Accepting various card payments:

  • International cards: Visa, MasterCard,
    JCB, American Express, Union Pay.
  • Accept 99.8% of local cards from 40 banks in Vietnam.
